  • NFT (Non-fungible token)

NFTs (or non-fungible tokens) are a special kind of crypto assets in which each token is unique as opposed to fungible assets like Bitcoin and dollar bills, which are all worth exactly the same amount. Because every NFT is unique, it can be used to authenticate ownership of digital assets like artworks, recordings, and virtual real estate or pets.

As we enter into the metaverse, we would love to connect our users across the globe with a more distinctive means of dealing with NFTs. With our vast variety of NFT dealers and bidders, Users can buy, sell, and trade NFTs on the platform.

How does CapitalXCH store my Crypto ?

CapitalXCH stores all of your Crypto in cold storage collaborative custody wallets, with the help of the team at Unchained Capital. Cold storage involves keeping the keys associated with the Bitcoin offline, whilst collaborative custody(also known as multi-signature) requires multiple parties to be able to sign a transaction to move funds from a Bitcoin address.Together, cold-stored collaborative custody is the safest way in the world to hold Crypto, and because CapitalXCH is a buy-side dominant product, it means we can maintain 100% of all Crypto in cold storage.
